**Q: What do you enjoy most about public speaking?**  
A: The **interaction with diverse audiences**! Each group brings different perspectives. Engineering students ask about implementation, philosophy students probe ethical implications, game designers wonder about creativity. These varied viewpoints enrich my own thinking.

**Q: What's the most common question you get?**  
A: **"Will AI replace humans?"** It's a deep concern for many. My answer: AI will change work, but it's a tool that augments human capabilities. The real question is how we adapt, reskill, and ensure AI benefits everyone - not just a few.

**Q: What's your most memorable talk?**  
A: **"Can Artificial Intelligence Fall in Love?"** at Uludağ University. It sparked an hour-long debate after the talk. Students from literature, psychology, engineering - all had different takes. That's when I realized the power of asking the right questions.
